Frequently Asked Questions

Will I have a place allocated with a Parclick reservation?

Yes, when you reserve with Parclick, a parking space is guaranteed for you.

How can I find out if a car park is secure?

Our platform provides security details for each parking location, including surveillance cameras and 24/7 security.

Do I have to leave my keys in the car park?

No, most locations allow you to keep your keys, but some valet services require it.

Can I access a car park with a trailer, luggage rack, or bicycle rack?

Some parking spots allow trailers, but it depends on size restrictions. Please check in advance.

Is it possible to reserve parking spaces for persons with reduced mobility (PRM)?

Yes, we provide accessible parking spaces. Please mention PRM requirements while booking.
