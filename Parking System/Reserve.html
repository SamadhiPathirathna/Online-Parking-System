Monthly Subscription Benefits

Unlock exclusive parking privileges with our monthly subscription, designed for your convenience and peace of mind.

Key Advantages

  • Guaranteed Parking Spot: Never worry about finding parking again. Your spot is always reserved.
  • Cost-Effective: Enjoy significantly reduced rates compared to daily parking fees.
  • 24/7 Access: Park anytime, day or night, within your subscription period.
  • Hassle-Free Entry & Exit: Seamless access with your digital parking pass.
  • Priority Support: Dedicated customer service for monthly subscribers.

Important Information

  1. Your monthly parking activates immediately upon successful payment and subscription registration. Your parking pass, issued on registration day, is valid for 30 days. Monthly renewal is required for continued subscription benefits.
  2. *Subscription Policy:* Monthly subscriptions cannot be cancelled before the next renewal date.
  3. Monthly fees are calculated based on selected parking hours and vehicle type. Note that fees vary depending on your vehicle category.
  4. A 5% service fee applies to all monthly subscriptions to support website maintenance and service enhancements.
  5. We advise subscribing 3-4 business days before your desired start date to ensure timely processing of your monthly parking access.
  6. For further assistance or inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact our dedicated support service center.
